# Bacon Apple Pupcakes

*Size*: Makes 2 dozen treats

- 2.5 cups of whole wheat flour or oat flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup beef or chicken stock
- 1/4 applesauce
- 2 Tbsps finely chopped bacon bits
- 2-3 Tbsp bacon grease (for glaze)

1. Preheat oven to 350
2. Mix together ingredients (except grease — set aside bacon grease)
3. Brush pan by brushing with butter or oil
4. Press dough gently into each well of the pampered pet pan, filling nearly to the top
5. Bake for 20-25 minutes until each bone is golden brown and fairly firm to the touch
6. Once cooled, drip grease onto each treat as a glaze
