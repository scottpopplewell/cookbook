# Peanut Butter Doggie Biscuits

*Size*: Makes ...

- 2 eggs
- 1/3 cup peanut butter
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1/3 cup corn meal

1. Preheat oven to 350
2. Brush pan with butter or oil
3. Whisk eggs and peanut butter together
4. Mix in oats and corn meal
5. Prease dough into pan with fingers
6. Bake for 18-22 minutes
7. Let stand for 10 minutes before inverting onto a cooling rack
