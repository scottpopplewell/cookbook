# 2020 Philidelphia Style

Makes a little more than 3 cups

- 100g granulated sugar
- 12g dry milk powder
- 1/4 teaspoon xanthan gum
- 45g corn syrup
- 350g whole milk
- 1 1/3 cups heavy cream

1. Combine the sugar, dry milk and xanthan gum in a bowl and whisk together
2. Pour milk into a medium sized pot and then add the corn syrup. Stir together
3. Add the sugar mixture and whisk vigorously until smooth
4. Set the pot over medium heat and cook, stirring often (do not simmer). About 3 minutes — Remove from heat once sugar has disolved.
5. Add the cream and whisk until fully combined.
6. Transfer to an airtight container
7. Chill overnight

## Notes

- Base can be stored up to one week in the fridge and frozen for 3 months
